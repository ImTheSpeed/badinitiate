Welcome to the University Event Hub!

This platform is designed to be the central hub for all university events, allowing the Student Union, faculty, and administrative members to post and manage events in one accessible place. Whether it's academic conferences, club activities, or university-wide announcements, this site ensures that events remain easy to find and reference over time.
